OUR fund

Omo VC strength lies in its diverse expertise, uniting innovative thinkers and specialists in independent teams to support founders effectively. We offer a range of funds from seed to venture to growth designed for ambitious entrepreneurs who seek both support and constructive challenges. By fostering collaboration, we empower founders to navigate their journeys and turn bold ideas into reality.

EVALUATION CRITERIA

The Seed team is committed to empowering entrepreneurial talent and seeks out the next wave of disruptors in Europe and around the world. They are not confined to specific industries, markets, or business models, focusing instead on identifying future leaders at the outset of their ventures. This approach requires a broad perspective and a genuine openness to the innovative ideas that talented founders bring to the table.
