The leading Investment Studio for Israeli Pre-Seed companies

our vision A new tailor-made model for Israeli early-stage growth

The AnD Studio model facilitates the validation of early stage investments, ensuring higher success rates and continuous growth for participating companies.

The Studio process allows each startup to emerge in the market on solid footing and equips them with the tools necessary to soar to new heights.

Provides office space for early-stage companies to collaborate with a professional team to grow their product.

30-day investment process with two paths:

$200K in 30 days

Accepted companies can receive up to $200K of investment with 5% in sweat equity in a 30-day process from AnD Studio’s uncapped safe. This path is tailored for very early-stage companies needing support with development and vision.

$500K in 30 days

Designed for companies with a developed product or initial traction yet short of a healthy seed round, this path offers a $500K investment with 5% in warrants in a 30-day process from AnD Studio’s uncapped safe. Once onboarded, we work diligently to elevate the company to the next fundraising level, enhancing their valuation.

Tailor-Made Process

AnD Studio does not operate in cohorts or batches; instead, it accepts one company at a time, providing a personalized process tailored to each company's specific needs.

No Valuation

AnD Studio invests in an uncapped safe, allowing them to bypass typical stressors associated with the studio process, such as raising capital and altering valuations.

No Graduation Date

AnD Studio has a start date but no end date. Companies can remain in the studio for as long as it takes to achieve necessary milestones.

Professional Support

Throughout the entirety of the studio process, companies receive continual support from the AnD team in market validation, product development, and all other aspects of their company’s formation.
