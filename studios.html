Our process:

customized approach

At OMO Studio, we take a unique approach by working with one company at a time, rather than in cohorts or batches. This allows us to offer a highly personalized process that is specifically designed to meet the individual needs of each company we collaborate with.

Professional Support

Our companies receive ongoing support from the Omo Studio team in market validation, product development, and all other aspects of their formation and market entry

No Graduation Date

We have a start date but does not impose an end date. We continue to support our Companies till they have a successful exit.

No Valuation

Omo Studio utilizes an uncapped SAFE for its investments, enabling them to avoid common challenges associated with the studio process, such as capital raising and valuation adjustments.
