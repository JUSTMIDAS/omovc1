ventures

omo ventures invest from early to growth stage in tech or tech enabled startups that :

  • Enable access to esential good and services such as education , healthcare, finacial services and consumer products and more.
  • Empower people through the creation of sustainable, large-scale employment opportunities.
  • Enable SMEs, people and Startups to grow and succeed by improving thier access to market & capital.

support:

We provide comprehensive support to our portfolio companies, combining financial resources with hands-on expertise from our dedicated team of operating partners and industry experts. Our goal is to guide these companies through critical stages of growth, from product-market fit to rapid expansion.

Investment Focus:

We are sector-agnostic investors, having backed businesses across a wide range of industries, including healthcare, logistics, finance, retail, food and agriculture, mobility, and the creative sector. Our investment extends across the different market, with a balanced portfolio.

diversity:

We invest diversly across the entrepreneurial landscape. Our current portfolio includes a wide range of businesses founded and led by individuals from diverse backgrounds. We prioritize a balanced investment strategy.
Looking ahead, we plan to allocate a significant portion of our fund to startups that are founded, co-founded, or significantly benefit underrepresented groups. By fostering an inclusive environment and supporting diverse entrepreneurs, we aim to create a more equitable and innovative business ecosystem.
